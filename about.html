The Concept of Auto4Sale

The concept behind Auto4Sale, which started in 2000, is based around convenience. This is why we have our tag line “Find it.Fast!”. Every service that we have to offer is based around convenience and this has been our secret to our success. One thing that we did realize is that, if used correctly, the Internet would serve as a very powerful tool with regard to making things happen a lot quicker and with more convenience.

Buying a vehicle can be a tedious and time-consuming affair, starting from locating the vehicle, the procurement of the vehicle and then driving the vehicle off the showroom floor. Auto4Sale is committed to making the first two steps a lot easier and simpler than what currently happens in the traditional world. We unfortunately cannot assist you in the third step as this is a privileged moment all car owners look forward to - My first drive in my new car. We do believe that by simplifying the first two steps you will appreciate the third step more.

Over the years the Auto4Sale team have built on the user experience making it quick, simple and more efficient to buy or sell new and used vehicles online, and with only a few clicks a car buyer has the opportunity of comparing as many vehicles as they like! Stacking up new, demo and used vehicles against each other comparing brand, model, price, year, mileage, power, engine, full service history, colours, features, comments and more!

Auto4Sale believes in creating long-term partnerships with our buyer/audience, enabling us to provide innovative solutions and an unsurpassed user experience. We are committed to service excellence, and we are continuously innovating and evolving as a business to present the ideal offering to any buyer visiting Auto4Sale.co.za.

Please feel free to get in touch with us on contact@Auto4Sale.co.za with any questions or suggestions.
